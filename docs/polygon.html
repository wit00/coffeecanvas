polygon.coffee

¶

Polygon is a graphical primitive of my design, created using inherit JavaScript/HTML5 functions. Polygon creates an n-gon determined by the sides argument and the radius argument.

 
class Polygon extends Shape
  constructor:(args = {})-> 
    super(args.x,args.y,args.rotationVelocity,args.velocity,args.otherVelocity,
    args.alpha,args.fill,args.stroke,args.lineCap,args.lineWidth,args.lineJoin,args.miterLimit)
    @radius = args.radius or 30
    @sides = args.sides or 5
¶

The polygon is drawn by rotating the canvas 2PI/(number of sides) radians and drawing a line radius length from the center.

 
  draw: (context) =>
    context.save()
    radians = 2*Math.PI/@sides
    context.beginPath()
    context.moveTo(0,-@radius)
    for side in [0...@sides]
      context.rotate(radians)
      context.lineTo(0,-@radius)
    context.fillStyle = @fill
    context.strokeStyle = @stroke
    context.stroke()
    context.fill()
    context.closePath()
    context.restore()
