star.coffee

¶

Star is one of my primitives. Star creates an n-star from a sides and a radius variable. The standard number of points is five.

 
class Star extends Shape
  constructor:(args = {})-> 
    super(args.x,args.y,args.rotationVelocity,args.velocity,args.otherVelocity,
    args.alpha,args.fill,args.stroke,args.lineCap,args.lineWidth,args.lineJoin,args.miterLimit)
    @radius = args.radius or 30
    @sides = args.sides or 5
¶

The star is drawn by rotating PI/sides and drawing lines radius and 0.5*radius out from the center.

 
  draw: (context) =>
    context.save()
    radians = Math.PI/@sides
    context.beginPath()
    context.moveTo(0,-@radius)
    for side in [0...@sides]
      context.rotate(radians)
      context.lineTo(0,-0.5*@radius)
      context.rotate(radians)
      context.lineTo(0,-@radius)
    context.fillStyle = @fill
    context.strokeStyle = @stroke
    context.fill()
    context.stroke()
    context.closePath()
    context.restore()
