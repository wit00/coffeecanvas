Square is a primitive that I designed. It's a modified rectangle. Like my rectangle class, its x,y coordinate marks its center rather than the upper left corner.

 
class Square extends Shape
   constructor:(args = {})-> 
    super(args.x,args.y,args.rotationVelocity,args.velocity,args.otherVelocity,
    args.alpha,args.fill,args.stroke,args.lineCap,args.lineWidth,args.lineJoin,args.miterLimit)
    @width = args.width or 100
   draw: (context) =>
    context.translate(-(@width/2),-(@width/2))
    context.fillStyle = @fill
    context.strokeStyle = @stroke
    context.beginPath()
    context.rect(0,0,@width,@width)
    context.fill()
    context.stroke()
    context.closePath()
